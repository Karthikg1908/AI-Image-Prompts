```txt
Ultra realistic cinematic scrapbook collage of a person (use uploaded face with 100% face match, no changes at all). Multiple rectangular photo panels arranged vertically and diagonally with slight overlap, creating an aesthetic layered collage. Each panel shows different shots: close-up face, mid portrait, necklace close-up, extreme eye close-up with strong shadow split, and a right-angle portrait with soft side gaze.

Styling: messy tied black hair with loose strands falling on face, soft subtle eyeliner, natural skin texture, delicate pendant necklace, off-shoulder white top. Add torn paper textures, flower stickers, music player card, handwritten notes, bloom glow, digital camera flash effect, ultra realistic.
```

```txt

• Cute playful expressions

Add hand-drawn white doodle elements:
• Outline around the main body
• Stars
• Hearts
• Sparkles
• Motion lines
• Small cute icons

Add aesthetic handwriting such as:
"shine"
"bright day"
"happy"
"smile"

Overall style:
Clean aesthetic composition, white sticker outline, soft pastel tone, glossy 3D chibi look, Korean Instagram aesthetic, ultra detailed, 4:5 ratio.
```
